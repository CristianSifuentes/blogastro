## Introduction

Social media should reflect the way you build. I use it to share product decisions, engineering lessons, and launch progress.

## 1. Share process, not only wins

Audiences trust founders who explain constraints, failed assumptions, and iterations.

## 2. Educate with technical clarity

Break down complex AI concepts into practical examples connected to real product value.

## 3. Keep narrative aligned with roadmap

Your content pillars should mirror what you are shipping this quarter.

## 4. Design for long-term trust

Consistency, transparency, and useful insights outperform short-lived trends.

## Conclusion

A strong founder narrative compounds when it is grounded in real engineering execution.
