## Introduction

Paid acquisition only works when product experience is strong. I focus on full-funnel systems: campaign intent, onboarding flow, and retention triggers.

## 1. Match intent to onboarding

Every campaign message should map to a specific first-use experience. AI personalization can adapt onboarding by user segment.

## 2. Use quality signals, not just click metrics

Track activation and retained usage, not only CTR and CPC. Revenue quality beats traffic volume.

## 3. Build experiment velocity

Create a weekly experimentation rhythm across ad copy, landing variants, and in-product journeys.

## 4. Close the loop with product analytics

Acquisition channels should feed product events into a shared model so you can optimize end-to-end contribution.

## Conclusion

Acquisition becomes efficient when engineering, product, and growth work as one system.
