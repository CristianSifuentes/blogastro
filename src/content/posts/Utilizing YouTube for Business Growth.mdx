## Introduction

Video is one of the fastest ways to communicate product value. I use it to teach, demonstrate, and shorten the path from curiosity to adoption.

## 1. Teach through product walkthroughs

Show the product solving real problems, not abstract feature tours.

## 2. Use narrative engineering

Each video should move from problem to architecture decision to practical outcome.

## 3. Build feedback loops

Comments and watch behavior reveal what users still do not understand.

## 4. Integrate with written content

Pair every video with a technical post so users can go deeper.

## Conclusion

For AI-native founders, educational video is a strategic channel for trust and market clarity.
