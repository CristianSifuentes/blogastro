## Introduction

SEO is still powerful when approached as product infrastructure. For AI-native teams, discoverability should be designed into the platform.

## 1. Build indexable content architecture

Use clean URLs, semantic structure, and internal linking that mirrors user intent.

## 2. Publish high-signal technical content

Answer real implementation questions with examples, tradeoffs, and reproducible steps.

## 3. Optimize performance and metadata

Core Web Vitals, structured data, and precise meta descriptions improve both ranking and conversion.

## 4. Connect SEO to product analytics

Measure how organic traffic activates and retains, not just how much traffic lands.

## Conclusion

Technical SEO is a growth channel when integrated with product and engineering workflows.
